Who We Are

Jesus Christ Saves Ministries (JCSM) was founded with a passion to reach the broken and forgotten in Granite City, Illinois and beyond—walking beside people and families through practical support and spiritual discipleship.

  • Established non-profit with years of community impact
  • Registered 501(c)(3) organization (EIN: [Your EIN Here])
  • Faithfully serving 100s of local families annually

Scripture foundation: “For God so loved the world, that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him will not perish, but have everlasting life.” (John 3:16)

Get Involved: Serve With Us

Step into life-changing ministry! Join us on 2nd Saturdays, 12pm-2:30pm at 1818 Cleveland Blvd, Granite City to serve the homeless, or get involved in youth nights, prayer teams, food drives, or outreach events.

  • Homeless outreach teams (every 2nd Saturday)
  • Youth nights (Fridays) – serving, safety, and mentorship
  • Door-to-door ministry and discipleship groups
  • Tuesday evening prayer walks and food collection
  • Weekly building clean-up/support
  • Donation drives for food, clothing, and special projects
