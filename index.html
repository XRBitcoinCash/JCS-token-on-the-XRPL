      JCS is not a replacement for faith, church, or Scripture.

🕊 Mission: Let Every Block Point Back to Christ


“For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also.” (Matthew 6:21) JCS is built to let every block point back to Christ and to give believers—including people who cannot openly practice their faith—a verifiable way to carry a sign of faith in their own wallet.


A Wallet-Held Artifact of Christian Faith
Through Sign the Ledger, a believer can create a prayer or testimony NFT held in the signer’s own wallet. It is intended as a token of faith in Jesus Christ: a durable digital artifact of Christianity that can remain with the believer even where open practice is difficult or unsafe.

❓ FAQ






Why a Christian token?



          To plant a clear testimony of Jesus Christ inside Web3,
          using transparent rails where faith, generosity, and accountability can be verified.
        






Is this a church or ministry?



          No. JCS is a technology and branding layer that openly confesses Christ.
          Individual believers and ministries choose how to use it.
        






Is a trustline safe?



          XRPL trustlines permit your wallet to hold a token.
          They do not give anyone control of your funds or keys.
          Always verify issuer details before signing.
        






How do I trade JCS?



          Trade JCS through XRPL DEX interfaces that show the correct issuer.
          Use the official Sologenic and XPMarket links here to reduce impersonation risk.
        






Is this financial advice?



          No. JCS is a symbolic and utility-focused project for believers.
          Prices are volatile. Seek wisdom, pray, and follow your own informed conviction.
        






What is the long-term vision?



          Persistent Gospel witness on-ledger, transparent giving mechanics,
          Christ-centered NFTs, and community tools that keep Jesus’ words visible in a digital age.
