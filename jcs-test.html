❓ FAQ

Why a Christian token?

To bring the message of Jesus Christ into new digital frontiers while supporting ministries, charity pools, and believers building on transparent, open infrastructure.

How do I start?

Set the trustline, connect your wallet, and hold JCS. Join community prayers, participate in cause voting, and support charity initiatives.

Is a trustline safe?

Yes. XRPL trustlines are a standard ledger feature. They don’t lock your XRP; they simply allow your wallet to hold JCS securely.

What’s the long-term vision?

A faith-driven crypto ecosystem: transparent reserves via escrow, charity distribution on-ledger, Christian art NFTs, and a global prayer network.

How can I support?

Hold JCS, share it with believers, and participate in governance and charitable votes. Every wallet and every prayer matters.
