1 · NFT proof

Your wallet mints an immutable NFToken. The URI contains a JCS testimony identifier, a SHA-256 content commitment, and a short excerpt. The full post is included in the signed mint transaction memos.

2 · Public registry

A separate one-drop payment to the JCS collector publishes the NFT ID and post fields into the shared feed. This second signature makes public discovery possible without giving the website custody of your NFT.

3 · Social interaction

Replies and Amen reactions are also one-drop memo transactions. The page groups them beneath their parent NFT or transaction, producing a blockchain-based social stream from public XRPL history.
